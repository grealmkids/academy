Mrs. Claire Ochola

Mrs. Claire Ochola is a multifaceted creative professional with a Bachelor of Arts in Music from Makerere University. Alfred Ochola

Alfred Ochola is a highly experienced Ugandan Full Stack Software, Mobile, and Web Developer with over 12 years in the field. An Electrical Engineer graduate from Makerere University, Alfred started coding in 2013 and began 3D animation in 2014, building a diverse portfolio of desktop and mobile applications, websites, and educational tutorials. He is also a seasoned professional graphic designer, with over a decade of experience working with leading organizations like World Vision and Monitor Publications. Alfred's expertise spans UI/UX, graphic design, 3D animation, app development, software deployment, and technical communication. He has designed over 300 fliers, created over 50 video ads, and designed over 20 company profiles. He has authored multiple books and articles, led the development of over 30 enterprise websites, and created advanced solutions like the Ecopharm CRM system and over 3 enterprise-level mobile apps. Known for his resilience and integrity, Alfred collaborates with a dynamic team of developers.
